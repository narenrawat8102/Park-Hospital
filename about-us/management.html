Our Management

chairman

Dr. Ajit Gupta is the Founder Chairman of the Park Group of Hospitals. His avid passion for subject of healthcare in respect of quality & affordability combined with his business acumen and innovative thinking is behind the meteoric growth of Park Group of Hospitals, making it among the fastest growing chain of Super Speciality affordable hospitals in Delhi NCR & Haryana After successfully taking Park Group of Hospitals to the path of success.

Dr. Ajit Gupta today spearheads the group’s strategic areas of expansion along with constantly guiding the team on new strategic initiatives. In a career spanning four decades, Dr. Ajit Gupta has a versatile experience in all facets of the healthcare industry, ranging from quality, affordability, technology. Dr Ajit Gupta has an unmatched track record of setting up and successfully running chain of super speciality hospitals . Being one of the pioneers in healthcare in India, Dr Ajit Gupta’s vision has made Park one of the largest healthcare service providers in Delhi NCR & Haryana with a large network of 8 hospitals and many leading doctors who have joined Park voluntarily.

Dr. Ajit Gupta has many recognitions in the Indian healthcare Industry to his credit. Under his encouraging and strategic guidance, the Park Group of Hospitals has shown stupendulous growth. A strong advocate of quality for healthcare, Dr. Ajit Gupta is one of the pioneers to offer affordable healthcare to the masses.

Apart from being a successful and a passionate professional, he is also a philanthropist. Dr. Ajit Gupta has been the social worker and an active member of NABH. He is also associated with various social activities for the benefit of the economically weaker sections of the society.

Dr. Ajit Gupta strongly believes that affordable quality healthcare has unmatched potential in India. Widely traveled and an avid thinker and with a strong belief in healthcare, Dr. Ajit Gupta is guiding Park Group of Hospitals through times of changing economic and climatic challenges, with his strong leadership skills and belief and insight in the future of healthcare.

managing director

Dr. Ankit Gupta has been the Managing Director of the Park Group of Hospitals since 2004. Under his dynamic stewardship, the Park Group of Hospital has experienced remarkable growth, making Park among the fastest growing chain of Super Speciality affordable hospitals in Delhi NCR & Haryana.

He looks after the key functions of the Group including revenue planning, corporate strategy, business development, human resources and corporate affairs. Holding M.B.B.S degree Dr. Ankit Gupta also has the academic insight for sound business decisions.

Dr. Ankit Gupta has to his credit of revamping the complete operations of Park Group of Hospitals. He has also been instrumental in consolidation, as well as rapid expansion of Park Group of Hospitals. This transformation has put Park Group of Hospitals on the forefront for excellence in the healthcare industry.

Known for his simplicity, charisma and clarity in thinking, Dr. Ankit Gupta has already earned the respect from various stakeholders, including the doctors community, government and patients.

Continuing on the footsteps of a rich legacy, Dr. Ankit Gupta’s vision is to make Park Hospitals as a preferred choice offering affordable Super Speciality services. His long term plans include to rapidly expand the presence of Park Hospitals in other parts of India also.
